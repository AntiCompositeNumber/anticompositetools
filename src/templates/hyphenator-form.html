Hyphenator

This tool will automatically hyphenate ISBNs and convert 10-digit ISBNs to 13-digit ISBNs using python-stdnum.

It only supports ISBNs in {{ '{{ISBN}}' }}, {{ '{{ISBNT}}' }}, and any citation with an |isbn= parameter. Magic links are not supported.
